Our Mission

The Antigle was created with a simple mission: to help gamers improve their skills and enjoy their favorite games even more. We believe that gaming is not just entertainment—it's an art, a skill, and a community.

Through our YouTube channel "Not Gaming Playz" and this website, we provide high-quality tutorials, guides, and resources that are accessible to everyone, from complete beginners to experienced players.

About the Creator

Hi, I'm Mukund! I'm a passionate gamer and content creator who loves sharing knowledge with the gaming community. What started as a hobby has grown into a mission to help thousands of gamers level up their skills.

Through Not Gaming Playz, I create content focused on:

  • In-depth gaming tutorials and guides
  • Tips and tricks to improve gameplay
  • Helpful tools and resources for gamers
  • Minecraft content and mod showcases

When I'm not gaming or creating content, I'm constantly learning new things to share with this amazing community.
